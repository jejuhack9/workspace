Motivation

Every year, the importance of environmental issues is rapidly increasing. We thought about how to handle food wastes that occupy a large part of them. One third of the food is now being abandoned and the cost of food disposal reach $800 million. So we will make good use of this situation and create a situation where both consumers and producers will benefit. Specifically, We will create a system where the restaurant owner uploads leftover food that can not be sold along with the expiration date to a web page, and that the necessary consumer buys at a discounted price or gets it for free. Restaurants that use these systems heavily will get credits, and there will be adequate compensation for them. This allows the store to gain credit, reduce the cost of disposing of food waste, and consumers can also get the food they want at a reasonable price.
